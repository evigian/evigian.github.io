About

Hi, this is me, nice to meet you!

I come from Greece and I live in Athens. I have studied various subjects while I currently study, between others, Audio Visual Arts at the Ionian University in Corfu. I love learning new skills, I enjoy travelling, discovering new places and getting to know different cultures and life-styles. This portfolio page was created for the FEND Google Developer Nanodegree Scholarship at Udacity and I will be updating it during the course.

So, stay tuned to see more...

Kapnikarea Temple

This was my first ever project on the web. I attended seminars about WordPress at the Department of Informatics and Telematics of Harokopion University in Athens. It was a great experience which made me eager to discover more about the tech world! The project was awarded from the Unit of Excellence of Open Source Software of Harokopion University. The content is my master thesis on Iconography and the pictures of the temple are published with a special permission from the Greek Ministry of Culture.

Animal Trading Card

This was my first project was created during the Google Challenge Scholarship. I learned a lot about HTML and CSS and I implemented my knowledge on it.

Pixel Art Maker

This was the final project of the Google Challenge Scholarship. It was an excellent chance to explore more Javascript and JQuery and it was fun putting this knowledge in action!

Greek Team Project

A team project by the Greek team of the Google Developer Challenge Scholarship. The idea started from a discussion on the greek channel of slack. Each student contributed with ideas or worked on the repository of the project on Github. Also, each one could contribute by making travelling cards. The project is open to anyone who wants to make a pin on the map with a favourite place or a travel destination!

E-Cook-Book

This was a team project which was made by Udacity students during the Google Developer Challenge Scholarship. The idea and the management of the project is of student I.Hajczewska. I had the pleasure to participate in the HTML team and to contribute a little bit!

Augmented Reality

This is a part of a project which was submitted at the Department of Audio Visual Arts of Ionian University for the course "Interactive Multimedia". The title of the project is "Tourism promotion article with Augmented Reality". Do you want to see more? Download the Aurasma App on your cell phone, follow my channel, scan the images and have fun!
